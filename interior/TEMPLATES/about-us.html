How it started

It all started when collage friends,Anuj srivastava and Ramakant Sharma, set upon their dream homes. It did't take them long to realise how fragmented the industry was and how factors like identifying the right professionals, coordination of tasks, and ensuring fair price and quality were pain faced by many.

Determined to fix this and offer a happier experience to homeowners, they built a much needed bridge for the industry. A.R architect was founded in 2018. It is now the trusted brand for complite home interior design and renovation for thousands of homeowners
